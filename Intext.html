Welcome to Northwest Cameroonians in Stockholm (NACS)

Frontend Developeical

North West Association of Camerronians in Stockholm (NACS) was formed in 2007. NACS is a non-political, non-pasrtisan and non-profit Association and brings together all sons and daughters of North West Province of Cameroon resident in Stockholm. the Objective of NACS are to Educate the general public on the culture and traditions of the North West Province of Cameroon. Create oppurtunities for dialogue between North Westerners and giving direction to cooperative intiatives between North Westerners and other regional, nationanl and international associations. Establish or encourage the establishment of information resources on the traditions and culture of the North West Province of Cameroon.

To all NACS members we must not permit permanent comfort in the success of today. We must not let the pleasures of now blur the issues of tommorrow.
